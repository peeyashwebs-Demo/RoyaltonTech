How To Fix Common AC Problems

Simple fixes for your most frequent air conditioning issues.

Your AC keeps your space cool and comfortable — until it doesn’t. Here are quick troubleshooting tips for common problems homeowners face.

1. AC Not Turning On

Check your thermostat batteries or circuit breaker. If those are fine, a professional inspection is needed.

2. Weak Airflow

Replace dirty filters and ensure vents are open. Blocked ducts may need cleaning.

3. Water Leaks

Condensate drain lines can clog with dirt or algae. Use a wet/dry vacuum to clear them.

4. Strange Noises

Banging or squealing sounds usually mean loose parts or worn belts. Call an HVAC technician to check it safely.

Conclusion: Regular cleaning and maintenance prevent most AC issues. For persistent problems, contact Peeyashwebs HVAC — your trusted cooling experts.
